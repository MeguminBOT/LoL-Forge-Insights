# Runaan's Hurricane

### Unique – Wind's Fury: 
Basic attacks on-attack fire additional bolts at up to 2 enemies in front of you, each dealing 55% AD physical damage. 
Bolts apply on-hit effects at 100% effectiveness and are affected by critical strike modifiers.
The bolts will target the closest enemies to you that are not the main target.

#### Strategy:
- Runaan's Hurricane is most effective when paired with on-hit effects that can be applied through its bolts.
- Runaan's Hurricane can also be used to increase the effectiveness of life steal since the bolts benefit from the stat with their damage.
- Runaan's Hurricane Runaan's Hurricane is highly effective in taking down multiple enemies at a time with its bolts.

#### Notes:
Wind's Fury:
- Wind's Fury deals basic damage, and thus will not trigger spell effects.
- The effect's damage is considered area of effect.
- Wind's Fury is an auto-targeted effect.

Wind's Fury's bolts:
- Deal the same damage type and apply the same hit effects as basic attacks would.
	- They apply life steal, trigger on-hit effects (excluding Vayne's Silver Bolts), can critically strike, draw minion aggro, and can be blocked or parried by effects such as Jax Counter Strike, Shen Spirit's Refuge, or blind.
    
	- They only critically strike if the primary attack would, except in cases of guaranteed critical strikes.
    - They do not trigger on-attack effects.
    - Are blockable Projectile projectiles.
    - Have a missile speed of 2000.
        - Ashe's Ranger's Focus and Kalista's basic attack missiles have a speed of 2500.

    - Does not target structures nor wards.
    - Deal 0 physical damage on-hit to the primary target, but can occasionally stack Black Cleaver (bug).
	- Wind's Fury damage is unaffected if the primary attack deals modified damage, except for Ashe's Ranger's Focus and Jinx's Fishbones.

- Wind's Fury does not trigger effects already consumed by the primary attack but will trigger effects not consumed.
- Shapeshifting champions can purchase Runaan's Hurricane but cannot trigger Wind's Fury unless in ranged form.
- Movement speed stat from Wind's Fury stacks additively with other percentage-based movement speed modifiers.
