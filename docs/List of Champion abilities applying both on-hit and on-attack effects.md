
# List of champion abilities applying both on-hit and on-attack effects

## Notes
The damage dealt by these abilities is not classified as ability damage, except in the case of Whiplash and the magic damage portion of Urchin Strike. Except in those cases, the damage dealt by these abilities will not benefit from Spell Vamp.
It is possible with Non-Damaging Abilities (above)—the implementation of this change could have been to have these abilities deal an additional 0  spell damage.
